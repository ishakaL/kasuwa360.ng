About Us

Welcome to Kasuwa360 – Where Your Market Meets the World

Who We Are

Kasuwa360 is a digital platform that connects local markets across Nigeria to the online world. We empower every vendor—from small kiosk owners to large-scale distributors—to showcase, manage, and grow their business online. Our goal is to bridge the gap between traditional markets and the digital economy.

Our Goals

  • Create a unified digital marketplace for Nigerian vendors.
  • Offer an easy-to-use platform for product listings and online sales.
  • Empower traders with e-commerce tools and digital literacy.
  • Enable local businesses to reach national and international customers.
  • Provide a secure, transparent, and affordable service for vendors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to digitize African commerce, starting with Nigeria’s most active markets. We aim to empower every merchant with the tools to grow and connect beyond borders—using just a smartphone and an internet connection.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every Nigerian trader can sell nationwide and globally without leaving their shop. Our goal is to become the #1 online marketplace in Africa for local and traditional market sellers.

Why Kasuwa360?

  • 360° Service: From product listing to payments and shipping.
  • Community Focused: Built for Nigerian traders, by Nigerians.
  • Youth-Driven Innovation: Founded by Ishaka Lawali, a visionary young developer.
